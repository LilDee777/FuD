How to Prepare Ghana Jollof

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 cups tomato stew base or blended tomatoes and peppers
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic and ginger
  • Chicken, beef, or tofu for protein
  • Stock or water, salt, curry powder, thyme, bay leaves, and oil

Steps

  1. Blend the tomatoes, peppers, onion, garlic, and ginger into a smooth sauce.
  2. Cook the protein in oil until browned, then set aside.
  3. Fry the chopped onion and add the tomato mixture with spices and stock.
  4. Stir in the rice and simmer until the grains absorb the flavor and become tender.
  5. Let it rest briefly, then serve hot with salad, plantains, or fried chicken.
